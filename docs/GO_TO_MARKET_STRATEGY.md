## Executive Summary

HDS Horses is a modern, multi-tenant SaaS platform for horse sellers, trainers, and breeders to manage their inventory, showcase horses professionally, and share listings with potential buyers through secure, customizable links.

**Target Market**: Professional horse sellers, breeding farms, training facilities, and equestrian businesses in the US and Europe.

**Business Model**: Subscription-based SaaS with tiered pricing based on active horses and features.

**Go-to-Market Timeline**: 3-month phased rollout (Q1 2026)

## Market Analysis

### Market Opportunity

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: 9.2M horses in the US, 7M in Europe
- **Serviceable Available Market (SAM)**: ~500K professional sellers/breeders/trainers
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: 5,000 organizations in Year 1 (1% of SAM)

### Competitive Landscape

**Current Solutions:**
- **Traditional**: Phone calls, PDFs, email attachments
- **Generic**: Google Drive folders, Dropbox shares
- **Competitors**:
  - Equine.com (listings only, no management)
  - DreamHorse.com (marketplace, high fees)
  - Custom CMS solutions ($10K+ setup)

**Our Advantage:**
- ✅ Purpose-built for horse sellers
- ✅ Professional share links with analytics
- ✅ Pedigree visualization
- ✅ Multi-user collaboration
- ✅ Mobile-first design
- ✅ Affordable monthly pricing
- ✅ No transaction fees

### Customer Pain Points

1. **Time-consuming**: Manual creation of sales materials
2. **Unprofessional**: Email attachments and dropbox links
3. **Tracking**: No visibility into buyer interest
4. **Collaboration**: Difficult to manage with team
5. **Security**: Can't control access after sharing
6. **Organization**: Scattered data across multiple tools

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Philosophy

- **Value-based pricing**: Based on number of active horses (inventory)
- **Transparent**: No hidden fees, no transaction fees
- **Flexible**: Easy to upgrade/downgrade
- **Annual discount**: 2 months free (17% savings)

### Pricing Tiers

#### **Starter Plan - $29/month** ($290/year)
*Perfect for individual sellers or small operations*

**Included:**
- Up to 5 active horses
- 2 team members (1 admin + 1 read-only)
- Unlimited images per horse
- 4-generation pedigree tracking
- Health records management
- Competition results tracking
- X-ray file management
- 10 share links per month
- Standard share links (time-based expiry)
- Basic analytics (views, last accessed)
- Email support (48hr response)

**Target:** Individual trainers, small breeding operations

---

#### **Professional Plan - $79/month** ($790/year) ⭐ MOST POPULAR
*For growing businesses and training facilities*

**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- Up to 20 active horses
- 4 team members (2 admins + 2 read-only)
- 50 share links per month
- All share link types (one-time, password-protected)
- Advanced analytics (geographic, device tracking, referrers)
- Share link expiry reminders
- Custom branding on share links
- Priority email support (24hr response)
- Phone support (business hours)

**Target:** Training facilities, medium-sized breeding farms, horse dealers

---

#### **Business Plan - $149/month** ($1,490/year)
*For large operations and multi-location businesses*

**Everything in Professional, plus:**
- Up to 50 active horses
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited share links
- API access for integrations
- White-label share links (remove HDS branding)
- Custom domain for share links (yourbrand.com/horse/abc123)
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority phone & email support (4hr response)
- Monthly strategy calls
- Training sessions for new team members

**Target:** Large breeding operations, multi-location facilities, corporate equestrian programs

---

#### **Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing**
*For organizations with unique requirements*

**Everything in Business, plus:**
- Unlimited horses
- Multi-organization management
- Custom features & integrations
- On-premise deployment options
- SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
- Dedicated support team
- Custom training programs
- White-glove onboarding

**Target:** Large breeding associations, franchise operations, international organizations

---

## Admin Dashboard Requirements

### Overview

The admin dashboard is a separate application for the business owner (you) to manage all organizations, monitor platform health, and make data-driven decisions.

**Access:** Separate admin subdomain (`admin.hds-horses.com`)
**Authentication:** Super admin role with 2FA
**Technology:** Same stack (React, Supabase, Vercel)

---

### Core Features

#### 1. Organization Management

**Organizations List:**
- Table view with search, filters, sorting
- Columns: Org name, Plan, MRR, Horses count, Users count, Created date, Status
- Actions: View details, Edit plan, Suspend, Delete, Impersonate

**Organization Detail View:**
- Overview: Plan, billing status, MRR, usage metrics
- Users: List with roles, last login, activity
- Horses: Count by status (Available, Sold, etc.)
- Share Links: Total created, active, expired, views
- Activity Timeline: Recent actions (logins, horse created, share link sent)
- Billing: Payment history, invoices, next billing date
- Actions:
  - Change plan (upgrade/downgrade)
  - Add promo credit
  - Suspend account
  - Impersonate user (login as them)
  - Send message

**Filters & Search:**
- Plan tier (Starter, Professional, Business, Enterprise)
- Status (Active, Trial, Suspended, Churned)
- Billing status (Current, Overdue, Canceled)
- Date range (Created, Last active)
- Usage (High, Medium, Low activity)
- MRR range

---

#### 2. Analytics Dashboard

**Key Metrics (Top Cards):**
- Total MRR

- Total Horses in System
- Total Share Links Created (Last 30 Days)
- Avg. Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Charts & Graphs:**

**Customer Growth:**

**Usage Metrics:**

**Plan Distribution:**

## Risk Mitigation

### Identified Risks & Mitigation Strategies

#### 1. Low Adoption / Slow Growth

**Risk:** Target market doesn't adopt as quickly as projected

**Mitigation:**
- Extended free trial (30 days if needed)
- Freemium tier (limited features, upgrade path)
- Money-back guarantee (30 days, no questions)
- Referral incentives ($25 credit)
- Case studies and social proof
- Industry partnerships (breed associations)

---

#### 2. High Churn Rate

**Risk:** Customers sign up but don't stick around (>7% monthly churn)

**Mitigation:**
- Proactive onboarding (personal calls for Business+ plans)
- Customer success team (quarterly check-ins)
- Feature adoption campaigns (email tips, webinars)
- At-risk alerts (low usage → outreach)
- Exit surveys (understand why they leave)
- Winback campaigns (special offers for churned customers)

---

#### 3. Technical Issues / Downtime

**Risk:** System crashes, data loss, security breach

**Mitigation:**
- 99.9% uptime SLA (Business+ plans)
- Daily automated backups (Supabase)
- Disaster recovery plan (restore within 4 hours)
- Status page (status.hds-horses.com)
- Incident response team
- Regular security audits
- Bug bounty program

---

#### 4. Competition

**Risk:** Existing players add similar features or new entrants

**Mitigation:**
- Continuous innovation (monthly feature releases)
- Customer feedback loop (feature requests)
- Industry relationships (partnerships, sponsorships)
- Content marketing (SEO, thought leadership)
- Product differentiation (pedigree viz, share link analytics)
- Network effects (referrals, integrations)

---

#### 5. Payment Processing Issues

**Risk:** Stripe account issues, high failed payment rate

**Mitigation:**
- Backup payment processor (PayPal Business)
- Smart dunning (automated retries, email reminders)
- Multiple payment methods (credit card, ACH, PayPal)
- Grace period (7 days past due before suspension)
- Payment method update reminders
- Fraud detection (Stripe Radar)

---

#### 6. Regulatory / Compliance

**Risk:** GDPR, data privacy laws, industry regulations

**Mitigation:**
- Legal counsel (data privacy expert)
- GDPR compliance from Day 1
- Terms of Service and Privacy Policy (reviewed by lawyer)
- Data processing agreements (DPAs)
- User data export and deletion tools
- Regular compliance audits
- Insurance (E&O, Cyber Liability)
